JEE Main 2023 session 2 exams are scheduled to be conducted from Apr 6 - 12, 2023, and the admit cards and the exam city intimation slip for the same are expected to be released soon by NTA in Mar 2023. Candidates can download their admit card by logging into the official website of NTA.

JEE Main Latest Notifications

  • Mar 20, 2023 : JEE Main Admit Card 2023 Session 2 is expected to be released soon on the official website.
  • Mar 19, 2023 : JEE Main 2023 75% eligibility criteria issue hearing at Bombay High Court scheduled on Apr 6, 2023.

JEE Main eligibility criteria 2023 deals with various factors like educational qualification, age limit, and age relaxation for reserved categories.

JEE Main Eligibility Criteria 2023 Highlights

JEE Main eligibility criteria were revised earlier to 75%, considering COVID-19. Hence, JEE applicants have requested in the petition that the conducting body remove the 75% criteria or lower it to 50%. The High Court was about to hear the petition on Feb 21, 2023. But, the court has rescheduled the date to Apr 6, 2023.

Earlier, Union Minister Dr Ramesh Pokhriyal 'Nishank' has waived the minimum requirement of 50% in PCM & an aggregate of 10+2. All candidates who have passed class 12 with PCM subjects/10+3 Diploma with Mathematics can apply for JEE Main Paper 2 (B.Arch). In addition, NTA has restored the 75% eligibility criteria for Engineering admission. Applicants must secure 75% marks in class 12 for the General category and 65% for SC and ST categories.
