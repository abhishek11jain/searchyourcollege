MBBS In Russia For Indian Students 2023-24

MBBS in Russia- Introduction

Russia has been one of the top destinations for MBBS in Russia for Indian students for more than a decade now. Standing at the prominent 8th position in the world in terms of the quality of medical education, Russia has been home to thousands of Indian MBBS students who are working as renowned and well-established doctors today.

The top medical universities in Russia have world-class infrastructures to support their high-quality medical education and comfortable accommodation facilities for Indian students. At present, there are more than 10,000 Indian students of MBBS in Russia chasing their dream careers to become excellent global doctors.

About Russia

Here are some interesting facts about the largest country of the world, Russia.

  • Moscow is the capital of Russia.
  • The official language of Russia is Russian.
  • Almost 30% of the Russian population speak good English.
  • The official currency of Russia is the Russian Ruble.
  • There is a gap of 2 hours and 30 minutes between Indian and Russian time.
  • Russia is the most developed country in the world.
  • The country occupies 1/8th area of the earth.

Frequently Asked Questions About MBBS in Russia
1. Can i appear in TS EAMCET if i belong to Bihar?

No other state student cannot apply for TS EAMCET as it is a state-level entrance exam conducted for candidates belonging to Telangana/ Andhra Pradesh. The conducting exam body of the entrance exam is JNTU Hyderabad. The eligibility criteria have clearly stated that the candidates applying for TS EAMCET must have a domicile of Telangana/Andhra Pradesh. If you are fulfilling the specified criteria, you can sit for the exam, otherwise, there are no chances. TS EAMCET witness approximately 2.16 Lakh applications per year.
